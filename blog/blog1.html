Let us praise Him with Gladness!

WORSHIP is obviously the best thing that can be done in a person's life. Some may think this is a waste of time and energy, but for those who live their lives for the Lord, this is the greatest thing that they achieved. To those saved by His precious blood, it is not a waste of time but a greatest honor. Recognizing what Jesus did in their lives, His death, His sacrifice and suffering for our salvation, the sweetest love story in the history of the world.

For all that He has given us, His Boundless Grace, His Unending Mercy and His Eternal Love. Let us enter His Court with thanksgiving in our hearts, shout of praises, voice of triumph and Give Him all the Highest Glory , Honor, Worship, Reverence.Let us fill with songs and dances the Temple of our living God now and forever.

GREAT IS OUR KING
